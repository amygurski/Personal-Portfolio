About

My transition into software development began shortly after moving to Cleveland after living in England for 8 years. I studied electrical engineering and worked in industrial automation, so the world of tech wasn't foreign to me. I had worked as an inside sales engineer and manager, specifying industrial instruments and leading a team of 30 people.

After moving to Cleveland, I couldn't resist pursuing my longtime passion for software development, soaking up many online learning resources and enrolling in Tech Elevator's coding bootcamp. With my attention to detail, knack for problem solving, and passion for learning, my charge into software development began.
